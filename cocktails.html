The Martini

  • Gin ~ 3.5oz
  • Dry Vermouth ~ Light Splash
  • Olives or Lemon Twist

    • Add Gin & Vermouth to mixing glass. Stir with elegant vigor. Strain into chilled coup and add garnish.

The Negroni

  • Gin ~ 1.5oz
  • Campari ~ 1oz
  • Sweet Vermouth ~ 0.5oz
  • Orange Twist

    • Add Gin, Campari, & Sweet Vermouth to mixing glass. Stir. Strain over fresh ice in rocks glass. Add garnish.
