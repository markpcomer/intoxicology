Cocktail Recipes

Elevate Your Mixology Skills

Martini

Martini

3ox Gin or Vodka

.5 Dry Vermouth

Skewered Olives or a Fresh Lemon Peel

Stir ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into chilled martini glass or coup. Garnish with a lemon peel or olives.

The Gentleman's Negroni

The Gentleman's Negroni

1.5 oz Gin

1 oz Campari

.5 oz Sweet Vermouth

Fresh Orange Peel

Stir ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into over fresh ice into a rocks glass. Squeeze oil from orange peel into the ice and place peel on top.

Manhattan

Manhattan

2 oz Bourbon or Rye Whiskey

1 oz Sweet Vermouth

Skewer of Brandied Cherries

Stir ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into chilled martini glass or coup. Garnish with brandied cherries.

Margarita

Margarita

2 oz Tequila

1 oz Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice

.5 oz Cointreau or Triple Sec

.5 oz Simple Syrup

Lime Wedge

Combine ingredients in a Boston shaker and shake vigorously until chilled. Strain over fresh ice into a salt-rimmed rocks glass. Garnish with a freshly cut lime wedge.

Lime Wedge

Moscow Mule

Moscow Mule

2 oz Vodka

.5 oz Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice

3 oz of Chilled Ginger Beer

Lime Wedge

Pour vodka and lime juice into Boston shaker and shake vigorously until chilled. Pour over fresh ice in a highball glass and top with ginger beer. Garnish with a freshly cut lime wedge.

Aperol Stritz

Aperol Spritz

.75 oz Aperol

Orange Wheel

Fill large wine glass with fresh ice and a freshly cut orange wheel. Add Aperol and top with equal parts soda water and prosecco.

Mojito

Mojito

2 oz Rum

.75 Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice

.75 Simple Syrup

Garnish of Lime Wedge

Add lime juice, simple syrup, and 5-6 mint leaves to a Boston shaker. Muddle the mint leaves. Add ice and shake vigorously. Strain over fresh ice & fresh mint leaves in a high ball glass. Garnish with lime wedge.
