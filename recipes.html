Cocktails to Impress

Martini

3 1/5 oz of Gin or Vodka

Splash of Dry Vermouth

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Stir until chilled. Pour into chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with Olives or Lemon Twist.

Gentleman's Negroni

1 1/5 oz Gin

1 oz Campari

1/5 oz Sweet Vermouth

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Stir until chilled. Pour into rocks glass over fresh ice. Garnish with orange twist. Thank me later.

Bee's Knees

2 oz gin

3/4 oz fresh lemon juice

1/2 oz honey syrup

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Shake until chilled. Pour into a chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Aviation

2 oz Gin

1/2 oz Marashino Liqueur

1/2 Creme de Violette

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Stir until chilled. Pour into a chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with brandied cherries.

Moscow Mule

2 oz Vodka

3/4 Ginger Syrup

1/2 Lemon Juice

1/2 Simple Syrup

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Shake until chilled. Pour into a Moscow Mule mug or a highball glass. Garnish with a lime wedge or wheel.

Killer Queen

2 oz Gin

3/4 oz Lillet Blanc

1/4 Benedictine

4 Dashes of Angostura Bitters

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Stir until chilled. Pour into a chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with brandied cherries.

Vesper

3 oz Gin

1 oz Vodka

1/2 oz Lillet Blanc

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Stir until chilled (I DON'T CARE HOW 007 LIKES IT). Pour into a chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Tea Rose Martini

2 oz Earl Grey infused Gin

1/2 - 3/4 oz St Germain

1/2 Ginger Syrup

1/2 Lemon Juice

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Shake until chilled. Pour into a chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with a lemon wedge or a skewered piece of candied ginger.

~ (In memory of Neil Simon Coleman)

Vader's Cape

1 1/2 Maker's Mark 46

1/2 ox Black Sesame Orgeat

Fresh juice from 1 whole lime

13 Dashes of Angostura Bitters

1 Pinch of Activated Charcoal

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Shake until chilled. Pour into a chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with a jasmine flower. Or a lock of Padme's hair.

Tom Collins

2 oz London Dry Gin

1 oz Fresh Lemon Juice

1/2 oz Simple Syrup

Splash of Club Soda, to top it off

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Shake until chilled. Pour into a highball glass, splash with club soda. Garnish with a brandied cherry and a lemon wedge.

Spanish G & T

2 oz London Dry Gin

4 oz Fever Tree Tonic Water

Pour gin & tonic into wine glass. Garnish with juniper berries, lemon wheel, and thyme.

Last Word

3/4 oz Gin

3/4 Green Chartreuse

3/4 Maraschino Liqueur

3/4 Fresh Lime Juice

Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.

Shake until chilled. Pour into a chilled coup or martini glass. Garnish with brandied cherries.
